Built on Open Knowledge

Queer Memory draws every fact, date, and image from the open Wikimedia ecosystem. Nothing is hard-coded: each page queries live structured data from Wikidata, labels from Wikipedia, and media from Wikimedia Commons.

The data is always growing. You can help fill gaps and tell untold stories by contributing to WikiProject LGBT on Wikidata.
