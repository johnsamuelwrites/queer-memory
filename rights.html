Rights & Law

Track the global landscape of LGBTQ+ legal rights, from marriage equality and decriminalization to anti-discrimination protections. All data is drawn live from Wikidata, following the WikiProject LGBT data models.

Discrimination & Violence

Wikidata documents patterns of discrimination (Q130297530) and violence (Q130297534) against LGBT people by geographic region. These entries help surface the human cost of legal inequality.

About this data

All data on this page is queried live from the Wikidata Query Service using SPARQL. The rights model follows the WikiProject LGBT data models, which define classes for rights topics such as same-sex marriage (Q130262462), decriminalization (Q130286663), and transgender rights (Q123237562).

Coverage varies greatly between countries. Western nations tend to have more detailed entries. You can help improve coverage by contributing to WikiProject LGBT.
