About this data

All data on this page is queried live from the Wikidata Query Service using SPARQL. Coverage reflects how well events, people, and movements have been modelled on Wikidata.

Gaps remain, especially for pre-20th-century records and non-Western histories. You can help improve coverage by contributing to WikiProject LGBT, expanding related Wikipedia articles, or adding media to Wikimedia Commons.

Built on Wikidata, Wikipedia, and Wikimedia Commons. Data is indicative and incomplete.
