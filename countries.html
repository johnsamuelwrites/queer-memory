Countries with LGBTQIA+ Records

Countries appear here if they have an LGBT rights entry or an LGBT history/timeline entry in Wikidata.

Counts reflect how many Wikidata items link the country via P17 or P921 for rights and history. The country profile page may show additional records across other sections.

About this data

Data is queried live from the Wikidata Query Service using the WikiProject LGBT models for rights and history on Wikidata.

You can help improve coverage by contributing to WikiProject LGBT, expanding related Wikipedia articles, or adding media to Wikimedia Commons.

Built on Wikidata, Wikipedia, and Wikimedia Commons. Data is indicative and incomplete.
